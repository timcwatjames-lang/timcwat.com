1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data. This site is primarily a learning resource. Here's what we may collect:

Information You Provide

When you use the Contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message. This data is sent via Formspree and forwarded to us. We use it solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not store it on our servers or share it with third parties.

Automatically Collected Information

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense may collect and process data about your browsing activity, device information, IP address, and location for the purpose of serving personalized ads. This data is collected and controlled by Google.

💡 We do not use analytics trackers, social media pixels, or any other tracking mechanisms beyond what Google AdSense requires.

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests and browsing history. It may share data with Google's advertising partners. Learn more about Google's ad practices →

Formspree

The contact form uses Formspree to deliver messages. Formspree processes your name, email, and message to forward them to us. View Formspree's Privacy Policy →

Google Fonts

This site uses Google Fonts for typography. When you visit, your browser requests font files from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. View Google's Privacy Policy →

3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser.

How We Use Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. However, Google AdSense may set third-party cookies on your device for the purpose of serving personalized advertisements. These cookies are governed by Google's privacy policy.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. You can also opt out of personalized advertising via Google's Ad Settings.

4. Your GDPR Rights

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data for advertising.
To exercise any of these rights, contact us. We will respond within 30 days.

5. Your CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Opt Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. Google AdSense may share data with advertising partners, which could be considered a "sale" under CCPA. Opt out below.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

To opt out of the use of your data for personalized advertising, please enable the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal in your browser, or use the cookie consent controls provided by our consent management platform. You can also opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

We respect the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends a GPC signal, we will honor it as an opt-out request.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Have Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy or want to exercise your rights, please get in touch.
